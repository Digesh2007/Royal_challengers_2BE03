Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Cluster One

Membership

How do I join Cluster One?
Joining Cluster One is simple! Visit the club office during office hours, attend any of our events, or register through our website. Membership is open to all students at S-VYASA.
Is there a membership fee?
No, membership is completely free! We believe in democratizing tech education and making it accessible to all students.
What are the membership benefits?
Members get access to: exclusive events, workshops, hackathons, mentoring, networking opportunities, project collaboration spaces, and job referrals from industry partners.

OD & Events

What is an OD (On Duty) application?
OD (On Duty) is an official leave granted by the college for student participation in approved events, competitions, or academic activities. It exempts students from regular classes during the event period.
How do I apply for OD?
Use our online OD application portal. Provide event details, dates, and supporting documents. Your coordinator will review and approve/reject based on event legitimacy and documentation.
What documents do I need for OD?
Typically required: Event poster/announcement, organizer's letter, proof of registration, and event schedule. Some events may require additional documentation like invitation letters.
How long does OD approval take?
Usually 2-3 business days. Urgent requests can be escalated. Once approved, you'll receive an official OD letter via email.

Events & Activities

What types of events does Cluster One organize?
We organize: Hackathons, Workshops, Tech Talks, Coding Competitions, Project Showcases, Networking Events, Industry Meetups, and Team Building Activities.
Do I need prior experience to participate?
No! We welcome beginners and experts alike. We design activities for different skill levels. Check event descriptions for specific prerequisites.
How do I register for an event?
Visit our website events page, click on the event you're interested in, and follow the registration link. You'll get a confirmation email once registered.

Technical

Can I access the platform on mobile?
Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices. You can apply for OD, register for events, and track applications on your mobile device.
What if I forget my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't receive it.
How secure is my personal data?
Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow best practices for data privacy and only use your information for club-related activities.

About Cluster One

Building Tomorrow's Tech Leaders

Our Mission

To foster innovation, collaboration, and excellence in technology by providing students with opportunities to learn, create, and share knowledge through engaging events, projects, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To become the leading technical community at S-VYASA, recognized for nurturing talented developers, designers, and innovators who make meaningful contributions to the tech industry.

Core Values

  • ✓ Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking and experimentation
  • ✓ Collaboration: Building strong teams and partnerships
  • ✓ Excellence: Pursuing high standards in all endeavors
  • ✓ Community: Supporting each other's growth and success

Our Journey

2020 - Inception

Cluster One was founded with a vision to unite tech enthusiasts at S-VYASA.

2021 - First Hackathon

Organized our first HackCluster with 200+ participants and 50 teams.

2022 - Expansion

Launched workshops, tech talks, and expanded to 500+ active members.

2023 - Recognition

Received Best Technical Club award from college administration.

2024 - Digital Transformation

Launched OD management system and digital portfolio platform.

2025 - Global Vision

Expanding partnerships and planning international collaborations.
